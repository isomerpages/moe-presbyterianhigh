**The Four Pillars of Physical Education**

<img align="right" style="width:150px;height:150px;margin-left:15px;" src="/images/PE3.png">


1\. Fitness Education&nbsp;  
Fitness Education focuses on developing students' aerobic and anaerobic fitness, as well as their health and performance fitness.


<img align="right" style="width:180px;height:150px;margin-left:15px;" src="/images/pe4.png">

2\. Health Education&nbsp;  
Health Education teaches the effects of exercise in the short term and long term, body composition and nutrition.

<img align="right" style="width:180px;height:150px;margin-left:15px;" src="/images/pe5.png">

3\. Outdoor Education  
Outdoor Education consists of the teaching of camp craft, orienteering and risk management.


4\. Sports Education  
Sports Education consists of the teaching of sports modules <img align="right" style="width:180px;height:220px;margin-left:15px;" src="/images/pe7.png"> in basketball, volleyball, badminton, football, softball, table-tennis, handball and floorball.


<img style="width:70%" src="/images/pe8.jpg">
encouraging teamwork and developing game concepts

  

**ASSESSMENT**  
Our assessment tools include:&nbsp;*   Fitness assessment includes NAPFA and selected fitness components
*   Sports assessment: theory and practical tests
*   PE Theory Test

  

**Enrichment Programmes**&nbsp;

To increase the ruggedness and tenacity of students, we conduct camps and work with external partners to facilitate sporting excellence.&nbsp;

Our key programmes include:&nbsp;

*   Secondary One Adventure Camp
*   Outward Bound School Singapore
*   Jogathon
*   Sports Fiesta&nbsp;
*   Sports Education Programme
