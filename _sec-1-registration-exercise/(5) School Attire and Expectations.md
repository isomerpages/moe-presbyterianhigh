<p>Every Wednesday, students are to wear the full school uniform, i.e. shirt/blouse
and pants/shorts/skirt.&nbsp; Students are allowed to wear half uniform
on other days of the week. Half uniform comprises the Polo T-shirt and
school pants/shorts/skirt.</p>
<p>School PE T-shirts may only be worn during PE lessons, CCA and other specified
school activities.&nbsp; PE lessons take place twice a week and students
should change into their school uniform after recess.</p>
<p>Students should also be neatly groomed.&nbsp; Male students should have
short and neat hairstyles, with a slope at the back and not touching the
collar or eyebrows.&nbsp; Female students with long hair must have their
hair plaited or tied up.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Ribbons, hair clips and hair
bands should be plain black or dark blue.</p>
