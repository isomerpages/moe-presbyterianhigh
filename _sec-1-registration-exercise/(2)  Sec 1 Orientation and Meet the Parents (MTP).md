<h3>Sec 1 Orientation and Meet the Parents (MTP)</h3>
<p>The Sec 1 Orientation Programme will take place on 2 Jan (Fri) and 5 Jan
(Mon) to welcome our Sec 1 students to Presbyterian High School. Their
orientation will culminate in an Induction Ceremony, together with the
Sec 1 Meet-the-Parents Talk on 9 Jan (Fri) evening. Students will also
celebrate as a cohort with a rousing Orientation Finale. Please find details
below:</p>

<p><strong>3.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Sec 1 Meet-the-Parents (MTP) 9 Jan (Fri)</strong>
</p>
<p>Parents are invited to the MTP to hear more about Presbyterian High School.
In addition, parents will get to witness the Sec 1 Induction Ceremony,
where we formally welcome the Sec 1 students into Presbyterian High School.</p>
<p>For parents who are driving, a convenient parking, waiting and pick up
location is at the carpark near block 634 (via Ang Mo Street 61), which
is next to the school. No parking is available in school.</p>
