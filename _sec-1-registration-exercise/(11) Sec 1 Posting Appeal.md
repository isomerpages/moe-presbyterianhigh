<h3><strong>Secondary 1 Posting Appeal</strong></h3>
<p>Primary 6 students who have just received their secondary school posting
in 2025 may use this <a href="https://go.gov.sg/phsappeal" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">form </a>to
appeal for transfer to Presbyterian High School (PHS). Their PSLE score
must meet the school's cut-off point of the posting year. Transfer will
be subject to available vacancies and the schoolâ€™s admission criteria.</p>
<p><strong>Notes</strong>:</p>
<p>1.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; The personal information provided in this form will
be used solely for the purpose of appealing for secondary school transfer
based on non-medical reasons.&nbsp; For appeals based on medical reasons
(serious medical conditions or severe physical impairments), please request
through your posted school by <strong><u>12pm, 22 December 2025</u></strong>.</p>
<p>2.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; The deadline for the submission of appeals is 29
December 2025, 12pm.&nbsp; Late submission will not be allowed.</p>
<p>3.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; School will not disclose reasons for selection/non-selection
of students.</p>
<p>4.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Students should still report to the posted secondary
school on 2 January 2026.</p>
<p>5.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; The application is deemed to be unsuccessful if you
do not hear from the school by 5 January 2026.</p>
<p>6.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Please note no hardcopy submission/documents are
required unless requested.</p>
<p>If any of the information provided in this application is in any way false
or incorrect, the application will be rejected.</p>
