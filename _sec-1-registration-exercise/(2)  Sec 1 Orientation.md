<p>*Notes:</p>
<p>1.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; The flag raising ceremony starts at 7.30am every
day. All students are encouraged to arrive in school earlier and be assembled
at the assembly square (or Hall on rainy days) by 7.25am.</p>
<p>2.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; The end time for students depends on the timetable
for the day. Please note that students taking HCL will end school at 1.30pm
on Friday.</p>
<p>3.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Half uniform comprises the school Polo T-shirt with
school pants/shorts/skirt. Please refer to the “School Attire and Expectations”
tab for further details.</p>
<p>4.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Co-curriculum activities (CCA) or academic support
programmes may continue after 3.00pm.</p>
