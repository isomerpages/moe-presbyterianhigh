<p>The Guzheng Ensemble puts their heart and soul into practising their 21-stringed
Chinese musical instrument. United in their pursuit of excellence, the
ensemble creates expressive and harmonious music and develops values, such
as teamwork, diligence and perseverance. Under the guidance of the Guzheng
instructor and teachers-in-charge, the ensemble achieves commendable awards
at the Singapore Youth Festival Arts Presentation every year.</p>
