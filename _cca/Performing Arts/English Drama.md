<p>The English Drama Club is committed to developing students’ abilities
in storytelling and artistic expression, and bolstering their stage confidence.
Our students learn to convey their thoughts passionately and explore different
modes of artistic expressions and storytelling techniques. The CCA unites
a community of kindred spirits, who find joy and meaning in engaging and
delighting its audience.</p>
